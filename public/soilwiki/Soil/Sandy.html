Sandy Soil

Information about Sandy Soil

Sandy soil is loose and has large particles, which means it drains water quickly and doesn't retain nutrients well. It's ideal for plants that prefer well-drained soil.

Soil Type (Acidic/basic/neutral)

Sandy soil is typically acidic but can vary depending on the region. It is important to test the pH level to adjust nutrient levels for crops.

Suited for these Crops

Crops like Carrots, Lettuce, Strawberries, and Peppers thrive in sandy soil due to its excellent drainage and ability to warm up quickly.

Regions of Occurrence

Sandy soils are commonly found in coastal regions or deserts. They are predominant in areas with hot, dry climates.
